The Partnership Fund for New York City is the $170M investment arm of the Partnership for New York City, New York’s leading business organization. The Fund’s mission is to engage the City’s business leaders to identify and invest in innovative New York City-based entrepreneurs—in the for-profit and nonprofit sectors—to create jobs, spur new business, and expand opportunities that enable all to participate in the City’s economy. As an “evergreen” fund, realized gains are continuously reinvested.
